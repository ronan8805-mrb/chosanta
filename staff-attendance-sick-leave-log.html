Staff Attendance, Sick Leave & Staffing Stability Log

HIQA Standard 6 · S.I. 674/2017 Reg. 15 & 16 · Organisation of Working Time Act 1997

HIQA inspectors examine staffing stability, continuity of care, and whether safe staffing levels are maintained. This log evidences attendance, absence management, agency use, and workforce continuity. It is reviewed monthly at governance meetings and forms part of the centre's Governance File.

Inspector Focus: HIQA will ask — "Are there sufficient staff, with the right skills, to meet the needs of the children?" and "What is your staff turnover rate and agency dependency?" This log answers both questions.
