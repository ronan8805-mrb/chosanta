Who We Are

Your Accommodation Coordination Partner

Chosanta Management is a dedicated coordination and support partner, helping public sector bodies, corporate organisations, and infrastructure contractors source and arrange suitable accommodation across Ireland.

We work with a network of independent property owners and licensed accommodation providers — sourcing, aligning, and facilitating solutions tailored to each client's requirements. We do not act as a landlord or letting agent.
