Physical Intervention & Restrictive Practice Record

HIQA Standard 3 · Tusla Restrictive Practice Register · TCI Model

⚠ CRITICAL: Physical intervention must only be used as an absolute last resort when all other alternatives have been exhausted and there is an immediate serious risk to the child or others. Every incident must be recorded on this form within 24 hours, reported to the PIC immediately, and entered on the Restrictive Practice Register. Tusla must be notified via NIMS for all physical interventions.

Regulatory Basis: HIQA National Standards 2018 (Standard 3) · HIQA Guidance on Restrictive Practice · S.I. 674/2017 · Tusla NIMS · Therapeutic Crisis Intervention (TCI) Model — Cornell University
