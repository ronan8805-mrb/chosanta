Governance Responsibilities

Audit-Ready · HIQA Regulation 23

1 Registered Provider
  • Ultimate legal accountability; maintains Statement of Purpose and governance structure
  • Ensures compliant workforce, staffing ratios, mandatory notifications (incidents, restrictive practices)
  • Oversees annual reviews, audits, and HIQA inspections; approves all policies
2 Director
  • Financial and operational oversight; leads annual strategic governance meetings
  • Approves quarterly reports from Governance Officer and PIC
  • Reviews audit findings; ensures risk register and service improvement plan maintained
3 Operational Governance Officer
  • Monthly internal audits and regulatory checks; manages training compliance records
  • Liaises with HIQA for inspections and documentation requests
  • Reviews complaints/incident logs; tracks safeguarding measures and improvement plans
4 Person in Charge (PIC)
  • Day-to-day leadership (HIQA Reg. 14); ensures care plans, BSPs, and assessments are current
  • Oversees recruitment, rosters, supervision records, induction documentation
  • Maintains daily logs, medication records, incident reports; reports to Governance Officer weekly
5 Frontline Staff
  • Follow personal plans, document interventions, complete daily notes and observation forms
  • Attend mandatory training (Fire Safety, MAPA, First Aid, IPC); receive regular supervision
  • Adhere to safeguarding policy; report concerns through designated channels
