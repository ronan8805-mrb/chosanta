Admissions & Discharge Log

HIQA Standard 2 · S.I. 259/1995 Articles 4–8 · S.I. 674/2017

Every admission and discharge must be recorded on this log. Under S.I. 259/1995, the PIC must maintain a register of all children admitted to the centre. This log is subject to Tusla ACIMS inspection and forms part of the centre's Governance File.

Statutory Requirement: Article 4 of S.I. 259/1995 requires a care plan before placement or within 7 working days. Emergency placements under s.12 must be notified to Tusla and have care plans expedited.
