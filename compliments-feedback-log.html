Compliments & Positive Feedback Log

HIQA Standards 1 & 2 · Quality Improvement · Positive Culture Evidence

🌟 Why This Matters: HIQA inspectors look for evidence of a positive, child-centred culture. A compliments log demonstrates that the service actively captures and celebrates good practice, listens to the views of children and families, and uses positive feedback to reinforce what's working well. This log should be presented alongside the Complaints Register to show a balanced feedback culture.

Record all compliments, positive feedback, and expressions of satisfaction from children, families, professionals, and other stakeholders. Reviewed quarterly at governance meetings and shared with staff to promote morale and reinforce good practice.
