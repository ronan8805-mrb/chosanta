Child Safeguarding Risk Assessment Template
This risk assessment is conducted in accordance with the Children First Act 2015 and is a component of the Child Safeguarding Statement. It identifies potential risks of harm to children while availing of our services and outlines the procedures in place to manage those risks.


This risk assessment will be reviewed annually or in the event of any significant change to the service or its operations.

--- TABLE 1 ---
[Risk Identified] | [Procedure/Policy in Place to Manage Risk] | [Person Responsible]
[A child may be harmed by a staff member, volunteer or visitor.] | [Safe recruitment procedures, Garda vetting, supervision, Code of Behaviour, DLP reporting procedures.] | [Manager, DLP]
[Inappropriate behaviour or language used by staff/volunteers.] | [Code of Behaviour, supervision, training and disciplinary procedures.] | [Manager, Team Leads]
[A child makes a disclosure and is not taken seriously.] | [Mandatory training, clear reporting procedure, DLP oversight.] | [All Staff, DLP]
[Children left unsupervised in communal areas.] | [Supervision policy, rota, visitor access restrictions.] | [Support Staff, Manager]
[Children exposed to online or social media risks.] | [Online safety guidance, restrictions on digital communication.] | [Manager, All Staff]
