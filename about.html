About Chosanta

Who We Are

A trusted property management company with a growing portfolio and an unwavering commitment to professionalism across Ireland.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Excellence

Chosanta Management is a trusted property management company with a growing portfolio across Ireland. We combine deep industry experience with a genuine commitment to our clients' needs.

We specialise in managing high-quality residential, commercial, and hospitality properties, while also supporting corporate clients and construction companies with scalable accommodation solutions.

Based in Drogheda, Co. Louth, we are ideally positioned to serve clients across Dublin, the airport corridor, and nationwide infrastructure projects.

Managing properties and people with the same level of care and professionalism.
  • Managing high-quality residential & commercial properties
  • Supporting corporate and infrastructure clients
  • Delivering flexible accommodation solutions at scale
  • Based in Drogheda — serving Ireland nationwide
