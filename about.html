About Chosanta

Who We Are

A dedicated accommodation coordination and property support partner, trusted by corporate and public sector organisations across Ireland.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Results

Chosanta Management is a dedicated coordination and property support partner, working with public sector bodies, corporate organisations, and infrastructure contractors to source and facilitate accommodation solutions across Ireland.

We work with an established network of independent property owners and licensed accommodation providers — coordinating, aligning, and supporting tailored solutions for each client's specific brief. We do not act as a landlord or letting agent.

Based in Drogheda, Co. Louth, we are ideally positioned to serve clients across Dublin, the airport corridor, and nationwide on projects of any scale.

Coordinating accommodation solutions with professionalism, reliability, and discretion.
  • Supporting HSE, Tusla, and government contractors
  • Coordinating corporate and workforce accommodation
  • Facilitating scalable solutions through our provider network
  • Based in Drogheda — serving Ireland nationwide
