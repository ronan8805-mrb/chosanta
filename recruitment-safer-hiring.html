Recruitment & Safer Hiring Framework

Section 1: Policy, Legal Basis & Personnel File Requirements

This framework governs all recruitment, selection, and onboarding at Chosanta Management. No person shall commence work with children or young people prior to completion of all mandatory pre-employment checks. This is non-negotiable and cannot be waived due to staffing pressures.

Legislative basis: National Vetting Bureau (Children and Vulnerable Persons) Acts 2012–2016 · Children First Act 2015 · Child Care (Standards in Children's Residential Centres) Regulations 2018 (S.I. 674/2017) · HIQA National Standards 2018 (Standard 6) · CORU (Health and Social Care Professionals Act 2005)

⚠ CORU Registration — Critical Deadline: The Social Care Workers Register opened 30 November 2023. From 1 December 2025, the title "Social Care Worker" becomes a legally protected designation in Ireland. Only CORU-registered professionals may use this title. All staff in Social Care Worker roles must be registered or actively applying before appointment.
