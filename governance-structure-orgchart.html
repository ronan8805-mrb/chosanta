HIQA Standard 5 — Leadership, Governance & Management

This document defines the governance hierarchy, lines of accountability, and escalation pathways for Chosanta Management's residential service. It is aligned to HIQA National Standards for Children's Residential Centres 2018, Standard 5 (Leadership, Governance and Management), S.I. 674/2017, and the Child and Family Agency Act 2013.

HIQA Standard 5 requires that the registered provider has effective leadership, governance, and management arrangements with clear lines of accountability. Inspectors will ask: "Who is ultimately accountable for this service?" and "What are the escalation pathways?"
