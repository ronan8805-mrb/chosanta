Maintenance & Repairs Log

HIQA Standard 5 · S.I. 674/2017 · Fire Services Act 1981 · Safety, Health & Welfare at Work Act 2005

All maintenance issues, repairs, and premises defects must be recorded on this log. The PIC is responsible for ensuring the physical environment is safe, clean, and suitable for children. Inspectors will review this log alongside their physical inspection of the premises. Outstanding high-risk items must be escalated to the Director immediately.

⚠ Safeguarding Note: All contractors and maintenance personnel must be supervised at all times when children are present in the centre. No unvetted person may have unsupervised access to any area. Contractor visits must also be recorded in the Visitor Log (CM-VLR-001).
