On-Call & Escalation Log

HIQA Standard 5 · S.I. 674/2017 · Emergency Governance

All out-of-hours calls, safeguarding escalations, and emergency management decisions must be recorded on this form. The on-call manager must complete this log for every call received and ensure it is reviewed by the PIC/Director on the next working day. This log forms part of the Governance File and demonstrates management oversight outside normal hours.

📞 On-Call Escalation Pathway:
Level 1: Shift Leader → Level 2: PIC (on-call) → Level 3: Director / Registered Provider → Level 4: Emergency services (AGS / Ambulance / Fire) & Tusla OOH
