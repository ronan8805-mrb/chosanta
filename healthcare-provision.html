Healthcare Provision Arrangements

Children & Young People

1 Overview

Chosanta Management ensures that every child and young person placed in our care has timely access to appropriate primary and specialist healthcare services. We support a proactive and person-centred approach to physical, mental, dental, and developmental health needs.

All healthcare is coordinated in partnership with the child's social worker, GP, and any existing services.

2 Primary Healthcare
  • Registration with a local GP within 72 hours of placement
  • Routine health checks and immunisation tracking
  • Dental and optical appointments scheduled as required
  • Health records maintained in the child's care file
3 Specialist Healthcare Access
  • Referrals to specialist services (paediatrics, CAMHS, occupational therapy, speech & language)
  • Coordination with HSE primary care teams
  • Transport and accompaniment to hospital appointments
  • Follow-up care plans documented and reviewed
4 Therapeutic & Psychological Support
  • Access to play therapy, art therapy, and counselling services
  • Referrals to CAMHS for complex mental health needs
  • Therapeutic input integrated into care planning
5 Mental Health & Emotional Wellbeing
  • 1:1 keyworking and emotional check-ins
  • Crisis support through Jigsaw or HSE referral
  • Mental health reviews included in care plan

Staff Training:

  • Trauma-informed care
  • Child and adolescent mental health
  • TCI de-escalation methods
6 Consent & Confidentiality
  • Informed consent obtained from social worker or guardian for non-emergency treatment
  • Young people are consulted on healthcare plans appropriate to their age
  • Health records securely maintained in line with GDPR and HIQA regulations
