Inspection Evidence Folder

HIQA / Tusla Inspection Readiness — Master Index

This folder constitutes the operational evidence base for Chosanta Management's residential service. It is structured to align with the HIQA triangulated inspection methodology: document review, staff and child interviews, and direct observation. All records are maintained in accordance with the National Standards for Children's Residential Centres 2018, the Child Care (Standards in Children's Residential Centres) Regulations 2018 (S.I. 674/2017), and Tusla's Quality Improvement Framework.
