3 Ethos & Function

Chosanta Management provides high-quality, person-centred residential care for adults with intellectual disabilities, autism, or associated complex needs. Our service is guided by the principles of respect, dignity, inclusion, independence, and evidence-based support. We empower each resident to live a meaningful life, integrated within the community, while receiving tailored support.

4 Admission Criteria
  • Adults (18+) with diagnosis of intellectual disability or autism
  • Admission based on compatibility with existing residents and centre's capacity to meet assessed needs
  • Full assessment and transition plan prepared with individual, family, and professionals
5 Care & Support Provided
  • 24-hour staffing with trained support workers; individualised care plans
  • Daily living support, communication, and social integration
  • Community activities, education/employment pathways
  • Medication management and health monitoring
  • Behavioural support; safeguarding and advocacy services
6 Management & Facilities
  • PIC: RNID or relevant qualification; Staffing: 2 staff per shift, 24/7 cover
  • Governance: Board of Directors led by Ian McKeown
  • Private bedrooms, communal kitchen/dining/living, sensory room, accessible garden, staff office
  • Fire detection/suppression systems, weekly drills, comprehensive risk register
7 Complaints, Advocacy & Policies
  • Complaints policy with easy-read formats; independent advocacy facilitated; logged per HIQA regs
  • Full HIQA-aligned policy suite: Safeguarding, H&S, Medication, Intimate Care, IPC, PBS, Service User Rights
