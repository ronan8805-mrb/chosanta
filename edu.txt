Education Arrangements for Children and Young People
Secure Living Support Services Ltd - Silverwings Centre
1. Our Commitment to Education
Secure Living Support Services Ltd is fully committed to the right of every child and young person to
access appropriate, consistent, and high-quality education. We view education as a core part of
personal development, future opportunity, and emotional well-being.
Education must be:
- Tailored to individual needs
- Consistent with national curriculum standards
- Holistically supported by staff, guardians, and professionals.
2. Access to Education
All children are assessed within 72 hours of placement. Depending on needs, we provide:
- Mainstream School Access
- SEN School Placement
- Alternative Education (Youthreach)
- Home Tuition / On-Site Tuition
- Remote Learning Support (iScoil, Scoilnet etc.)
3. Daily Support from Care Staff
- Morning routines and transport
- Homework supervision
- School communication
- Representation at meetings as loco parentis
4. Special Educational Needs (SEN)
- Access to educational assessments and therapies
- Development of IEPs
- One-to-one or classroom supports
5. Monitoring & Review
- Monthly Education Plan reviews
- Progress recorded in Care Plans
- Daily logging and engagement strategies
6. Values and Holistic Learning
- Promotion of emotional resilience
- Life skills development
- Creative expression opportunities
