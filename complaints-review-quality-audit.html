Complaints Log & Monthly Review

S.I. 674/2017 Regulation 20 · HIQA National Standards 2018

All complaints must be logged on receipt and acknowledged within 5 working days. A full response must be provided within 10 working days. Children, families, advocates, and anonymous complainants all have equal access to this process. Inspectors review both the log and the quality of the response.

Quarterly Quality Audit

Internal Self-Assessment — Tusla Quality Improvement Framework · HIQA National Standards 2018

This internal audit is completed by the PIC quarterly. It maps directly to the HIQA National Standards and the Tusla Quality Improvement Framework. Scoring: C = Compliant · SC = Substantially Compliant · NC = Non-Compliant. Non-compliant findings must be entered on the Quality Improvement Plan (QIP) with a time-bound action.
